12. Final Founder Advice

Year 1 Focus: Survival & QC

Forget becoming Adani for 365 days. Focus on one thing: Quality Consistency. If your second shipment tastes different from your first, you are dead in the export market. Sleep on the factory floor if you have to.

What to Ignore

Ignore vanity metrics like Instagram likes or fancy office furniture. Ignore low-margin local wholesalers who demand 90 days credit. Cash flow is oxygen.

The Difference

Small brands sell powder. Empires sell Supply Chain Security. Your biggest clients (Nestle, Unilever, D-Mart) don't care about your logo; they care that you never run out of stock and never have metal particles in the powder.

"You don't build a 100 Crore brand by shouting. You build it by shipping perfectly, every single time, for a decade."
